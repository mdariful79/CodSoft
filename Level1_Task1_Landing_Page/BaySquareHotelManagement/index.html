Our Amenities

The Infinity Pool

The infinity pool on level four has the most stunning view of the ocean as you can view the vast ocean over the edge of the pool giving you a feeling that you are right in the middle of the ocean.

Tararom SPA

Leaving behind the pressure of everyday life, you enter a world where time stands still, and relaxation pervades. Our Authentic Thai Spa offers the perfect retreat: be it to heal, pamper, rejuvenate or revitalize, rest assured that your desire will be met, completely. Drawing on ancient rituals with modern day touches, the unique Tararom Spa treatment menu merges old with new to create luxurious treatments that recharge your body`s essential energies. Leave your worries somewhere for a moment and let your spa journey begin!

Cafe'79

As the name of the bar suggests you can view unobstructed most beautiful sunsets sitting in the evening at the extended bar deck overlooking the beach and the ocean. We welcome you to immerse into the relaxed elegance of the Cafe'79. By night, moods lift and music embraces as delicious tapas and Asian small plates are paired with global beer, wine, whiskey and cognac selections, as well as creative cocktails with an Asian twist. Whether for an aperitif, dining experience al fresco or evening among friends, The Cafe'79 presents an elegant backdrop for whatever the day or night may bring. Enjoy live music by local band and house DJ spinning lounge music every Thursday through Saturday, 8:00pm to midnight.

Conference Room

>Marina Ball room is 4338 sq.ft. grand ball room perfectly suitable for MICE and private functions. It can accommodate up to 500 participants in theater style and 450 in banquet style.

The Life Style Gym

Be a regular gym lover or not you can enjoy a quick work out session in the Life Style Gym at the pool side. Luxury of Private Steam & Sauna suites to stimulate after exercise relaxation.
